About Our Company

Learn more about our history, vision, and mission

Our Story

Starting from food service sectors since 1992, PT Pandurasa Kharisma continue to expand into retails. With line of products ranging from 45 international brands such as USA, Canada, Switzerland, France, Germany, Denmark, New Zealand, Australia, Singapore & Malaysia.

Our sales team is able to leverage existing relationships to efficiently spread our products to a wide customer base includes leading luxury and business hotels, restaurant chains and stand alone outlets, and supermarket chains.

Headquartered in Jakarta we are well positioned to distribute our product range to the largest markets in Indonesia. To further widen the scope of our distribution we have partners in other large cities across Indonesia, we continue to look for ways to increase the spreading of our products.

Our Vision

To be the leading food and beverage distribution company in Indonesia, recognized for our exceptional product portfolio and outstanding service.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality international food and beverage products to the Indonesian market through innovative distribution strategies and strong partnerships with global brands.
